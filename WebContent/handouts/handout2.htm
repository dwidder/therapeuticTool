What is the ImPACT test?

·       The ImPACT (Immediate Post-Concussion Assessment and Cognitive Testing) is a widely used COMPUTERIZED TEST FOR CONCUSSION

 

                 

 

·       ImPACT is used by athletes on sports teams, high schools, colleges and universities, clinical centers and military units to measure potential changes following a concussion.

 

 

·       Measures VERBAL AND VISUAL MEMORY, ATTENTION, SPEED OF PROCESSING and REACTION TIME

 

·       Test takes about 25 MINUTES TO COMPLETE on a computer

 

·       If you are an athlete, the pre-concussion an ImPACT test given before an incident can establish baseline cognitive functioning that can be useful if you SUFFER A CONCUSSION LATER

 

·       Comparing the pre- and post-concussion test scores can help determine when you are ready to RETURN TO ACTIVITES

 

 

What is cognitive rest?(If you are on strict cognitive rest, have a friend read this handout to you)

 

The purpose of cognitive rest is to give the brain a chance to recover. Research suggests that initial time off from all activities and increased sleep speeds recovery.

                                       

 

·       Time off from school or work

·       No homework

·       No reading

 

          

 

·       No screen use (computers, video games, texting, use of cell phones, limited or no television)

 

          

 

·       No exercise, athletics, chores that result in perspiration/exertion

·       No trips, social visits in or out of home

·       Allow for increased rest and sleep

 (Moser, Glatts & Schatz, 2012)
